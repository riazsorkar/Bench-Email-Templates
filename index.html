Dear [Company Admin Name],

Thank you for registering your company on OutsourceToBD!

We noticed you haven't posted a bench listing yet. If you're unsure what it is or whether it's right for you — this email is for you.

What is a Bench Listing?

In IT outsourcing, "bench" means a developer who is currently free — project ended, deal delayed, or partially utilized. They're skilled and on payroll, but not billing anyone.

A bench listing is simply you saying: "I have a developer available — is anyone interested?" — completely anonymously. No one knows it's your company.

Why Does It Matter?

A senior developer on bench costs $3,000–5,000/month with zero revenue. Even one placement through Bench Exchange can recover weeks of that cost — and it takes less than 5 minutes to post.

How It Works

1
 Post anonymously — Your company appears as an alias like "Company #A3F2." Fill in role, skills, experience level, rate, and availability.
2
 Receive Knocks — Interested companies must agree to an NDA before contacting you. You see who they are. They don't see you yet.
3
 You stay in control — Accept or reject every knock. Nobody gets access without your approval.
4
 Share a Blind CV (Optional) — If you've uploaded one, they see a redacted resume — no name, no contact info.
5
 Full Reveal — Only when both sides agree, identities are exchanged. Deal happens directly between you.

Is This Safe?

✅ Company name never public
✅ NDA before any contact
✅ No details without approval
✅ Pause or close anytime

Who Should Post?

Any company with even one developer who is between projects, available in the next 2–4 weeks, or partially free. All levels welcome — Junior, Mid, Senior, Lead, and Principal.

Post Your First Listing — Free

Log in → Bench Exchange → Post New Listing → Done in 5 minutes

Have questions? Just reply to this email — we're happy to help.

P.S. — Prefer a hands-off start? Just reply with your bench details and we'll post the listing for you. Zero effort on your end.
